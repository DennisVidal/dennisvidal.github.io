Hey there and welcome to my Portfolio

About Me

I'm a passionate game developer that recently graduated with distinction from the University of Applied Sciences in Kempten, with a bachelor's degree in Game Engineering as well as a master's degree in Game Engineering and Visual Computing.
Playing and developing interesting games which allow me to solve challenging problems or create immersive experiences is what I find joy in. My interests lie especially in procedural generation and all sorts of gameplay related mechanics and features.

My Skills

C / C# / C++

C++ was the main language used in almost all programming related university lectures, with some of the few exceptions being bigger projects which utilised Unity and therefore C#. This allowed me to gather a good amount of programming experience in both languages.

Engine Knowledge

Unity is the engine I have most experience in, as it was used extensively throughout university, but I also have a decent amount of experience with Unreal Engine and some basic experience with Cryengine.

Game Development

Thanks to my bachelor's degree, master's degree, modding experience, and partially thanks to my years of playing games, I'm familiar with many fields essential to game development, ranging from modelling and animation to design and graphics and of course programming.

Working in Groups

During university I had the pleasure of being involved in a plethora of group projects of varying sizes. These projects taught me important teamwork related skills, such as how to communicate and solve problems within a team.

Noteworthy Projects

Research Ravine

A flight simulation that was created as part of my master thesis. It utilises isosurface extraction and procedural generation in combination with compute shaders, VR and an Icaros system to deliver an immersive and unique experience with every play session.

An application that is the result of my bachelor thesis. It allows for the interaction with a character through a row of nonverbal means, such as eye contact and distance to tthe character. The game makes use of AR via a HoloLens to embed the character in the real world.
